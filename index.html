Additional information

Tech4change is an initiative for transforming young lives through the power of technology by providing free mentorship to it's participants.

participants are given basic training in various fields of technology while being encourage to learn on their own also and to channel all questions and difficulties to the community for solution. individuals with same field of interest are grouped together and assigned a mentor to that effect. Presently we use whatsApp as our platform while trainings are performed via zoom meetings and tutorials are uploaded on youtube.
